Our Story

Rest Well at the
Heart of Hills

Nestled in the misty embrace of the Nilgiri Mountains at 6,500 feet above sea level, Hotel Silver Oak is Ooty's finest budget retreat. Just 250 metres from Charing Cross and 1 km from the railway station, we place you at the very pulse of this legendary hill station.

From our contemporary-styled Deluxe and Executive rooms to our charming private cottages, every space is designed with warmth, comfort, and the spirit of the Blue Mountains in mind. We also offer exclusive Nilgiri products — teas, dry fruits, spices, and natural oils — as cherished souvenirs of your stay.
